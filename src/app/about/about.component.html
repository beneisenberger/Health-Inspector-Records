Health Inspector is a small record label in Omaha, Nebraska trying to put our small corner of the world out into the universe and our releases into as many ears and hands as possible.

Formed in 2013, mostly to release our own releases - we have put out and promoted a small stream of cassettes, CDs and digital releases, with our first vinyl release set for later in 2020.

Q: Do you accept demos?
A: Sure, why not? I'm just one person with a few friends. If you want to tag-team some funding and PR for an album release, if your stuff is great, I'd love to help. Email me at healthinspectorrecords@gmail.com
