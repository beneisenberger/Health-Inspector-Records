// about us //


Health Inspector is a small label - or more accurately, a collective of musicians - from Omaha, Nebraska. Originally conceived as a mock-band name while he was in high school, guitarist Ben Eisenberger took up the moniker to release his and friends' music through a single outlet in 2013. Health Inspector's cassettes are all dubbed and assembled in Omaha, while CDs and (soon-to-be) vinyl is outsourced.
