Health Inspector is a small record label in Omaha, Nebraska, USA trying to get our small corner of the world out into the universe and our releases into as many ears and hands as possible.

Formed in 2016, mostly to put out our own releases - we have distributed and promoted a small stream of cassettes, CDs and digital releases, with our first vinyl release set for later on in 2020.
