Hussies

Long-standing Omaha rock band with a wide breadth of output spanning styles like hardcore punk and straight-up rock songwriter guitar jams.

Screaming Plastic

Collective of Omaha and San Francisco-based improvisers, creating soundscapes ranging from the ambient-and-open to dissonant-and-overbearing. New album sponsored by a New Music USA project grant.

FiFi NoNo

Hardcore punk-meets-industrial metal, angular riffs, washed out vocals and an overall heavy aesthetic. Coming soon-to-be-back from the dead.

Melancholy and forelorn fingerpicking folk from the weird dude in the corner.

Country-tinged folk by the lead force behind Hussies. Explores love, loss and introspection with a bit of wit and appreciation of the beauty of nature.
